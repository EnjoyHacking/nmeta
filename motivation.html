Motivation

The nmeta project is motivated by a real world problem in enterprise networks. The rapidly increasing number and types of networked devices is challenging the effectiveness of traditional static traffic classification techniques. The sheer volume of flow types is threatening the viability of static classification techniques that require network operators to understand the characteristics of each flow type.

Traffic classification is the allocation of packets to types, and in enterprise networks this is commonly used as an input into Quality of Service (QoS) treatment
